# Ordering Milk

How customers place one-time milk orders on the Shree Shyam Dairy Farm storefront.

## Steps

1. Browse products on the home page or product catalog
2. Add items to cart (quantity, size)
3. Proceed to checkout
4. Enter delivery address (saved addresses available when logged in)
5. Pay via Razorpay (UPI, card, net banking)
6. Receive order confirmation and tracking in **Account → Orders**

## Guest checkout

You can order without an account. Create an account later to view order history and save addresses.

## Payment methods

- UPI (QR and intent)
- Credit / debit cards
- Net banking

Powered by Razorpay. Payment status is verified server-side before order confirmation.

## Delivery

Orders are assigned to delivery staff. Track status in your account or via the [Mobile App](./mobile-app.md).

## Need help?

Use the chat assistant on the storefront or contact via the contact form.
